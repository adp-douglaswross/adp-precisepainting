# Executive Summary: Precise Painting Operations Platform

## Business Outcome

Precise Painting will run one integrated operating system for field and HQ teams, replacing fragmented quoting, dispatch, timesheet, payroll, and invoicing workflows with a unified process.

## Delivered Experience

- **Painter route (`/mobile`)**: 4-step flow for receive/start, quote+variation+materials, execute+time/expense, complete+submit.
- **HQ route (`/hq`)**: 4-step flow for intake/approval, dispatch+message+reassign, monitor+timesheets/payroll, invoice+analytics.
- **Workforce board**: 50 painter status view with action controls.
- **AI assistance**: present in every step for recommendations, validation, and next-action guidance.

## Why this design

The design follows leading global field-service best practices: role-specific workflows, standardized lifecycle states, live operational visibility, and human-in-the-loop AI support.

## Customer Discussion Points

1. Finalize KPI targets (quote cycle, correction rate, invoice timeliness).
2. Confirm operational policies (reassignment rules, overtime, approval thresholds).
3. Confirm invoice/finance integration requirements.
4. Approve rollout plan (pilot cohort -> full 50 painter deployment).
